Welcome to Ubisoft Paris

We are committed to success, to the desire to constantly exceed our limits and make a difference.

We have the resources to back our ambitions and a wealth of opportunities. Our determination to achieve excellence is what allows us to transform our ideas into reality. Together, we make history. We constantly push the limits of art, technology and storytelling. Our entrepreneurial processes and our resources encourage innovative ideas. Our teams’ agility enables each individual to exceed their limits and deliver the most inspiring worlds possible to our players.

Meet the Rabbids

Rabbid Peach
Rabbid Peach is a Rabbid dressed as Princess Peach. She was created when a Rabbid was fused with a Princess Peach wig in a lab.
 
Rabbid Yoshi
Rabbid Yoshi is a Rabbid dressed as Yoshi. Rabbid Yoshi teams up with Mario to save the Mushroom Kingdom from Rabbid-fused enemies and Bowser Jr.'s attempted takeover.
 
Rabbid Mario
Rabbid Mario is a Rabbid dressed as Mario. Rabbid Mario teams up with Mario to save the Mushroom Kingdom from Bowser Jr.
 
Rabbid Luigi
Rabbid Luigi is a Rabbid dressed as Luigi. Rabbid Luigi teams up with Luigi to save the Mushroom Kingdom from Bowser Jr.
